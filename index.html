Happy One Year

For my beautiful girlfriend, Galaletsang

🌹

One year. Twelve months. Every single day.

The past twelve months have been so memorable.
Some moments were captured. Many were not.
But every single one of them remains a part of me.

As you scroll through this, I want to remind you —
just how beautiful our love is.

Cold Nights in the Car

From the cold nights chilling in the car because you were not ready to host me. We just sat there, engine off, the cold creeping in, talking and laughing, doing absolutely nothing and somehow making it feel like everything. I adore those nights. I would not trade a single one of them.

The Day I Finally Asked

Finally asking you to be my girlfriend was a moment I had been carrying quietly for a while. When I finally said the words and you said yes, something settled in me. Something that has not moved since.

When I Was Battling the Flu

Having you around when I was battling the flu was beautiful. It shows how supportive and caring you are. That kind of love is rare, Galaletsang, and I do not take it for granted for a single second.

Random Days & Beautiful Smiles

I adore your smile and every moment we spent together. The random days with beautiful smiles, the quiet and unplanned days that turned into some of my favourite memories with you.

Friday Afternoon Drives

Picking you up from work on a Friday afternoon and driving around. The music, the conversation, the easy quiet between us. Those drives felt like freedom. They still do.

Our Birthdays Together

Spending our birthdays together meant more than I could put into words at the time. Celebrating you and being celebrated by you is one of those things I want to keep doing for a very long time.

December Holidays & Park Visits

Spending our December holidays together. Our park visits. Long afternoons with nowhere to be and no rush to get there. Those moments of just existing together, unhurried and unbothered, are the ones I will hold the longest.

Every Moment, Perfect or Not

I love all of it — the great moments and the imperfect ones. I love you when we are happy and I love you when we are sad. I love you in everything, in all the in-between spaces that no one else sees. All of it is ours, and I would not change a single thing.

You could name them all, my love.
We have shared so many special memories
and there are so many more to come.

I love you so much, Galaletsang.

With great love

Lesego
